    What is WordPress? Complete WordPress Tutorial

    WordPress is a web publishing software you can use to create your own website or blog. It is very easy and flexible enough to make different types of websites. That’s the main reason why WordPress has grown so much in popularity. Since it was released in 2003, WordPress has become one of the most well-known web publishing platforms.

    Most of the people think that it is just a blogging platform. Yet, WordPress has started as a blogging tool, and then it has evolved throughout the years into a powerful website builder and a content management system (CMS).

    It also empowers you to construct and deal with your own full-featured site utilizing only your web browser. If you have used a content tool like Microsoft Word, then you will be comfortable with the WordPress Editor.

    It is free software. And it means that you are free to use and modify your site from this software to match your requirements. You can use this software to create any kind of site. While the software is free, you only need a domain name and web hosting to set up.

    A domain name is the address of your site on the internet. This is the thing that your clients type in their browser’s address bar to access your site (for example, wpuser.com).

    Types of WordPress Sites

    Here are some of the examples of the types of WordPress websites that you can make with WordPress.org are:-

    • E-commerce site – This E-commerce site will allow you to sell the goods or services online and get your payment via an online system. You can download and install this plugin to extend the default functionality so you can also have an online store on your WordPress site.
    • Business site – Many businesses get lots of benefit from having an online form of their own site. From this type of site, Customers can contact you, ask for a quote, schedule an appointment, and much more. And if your business also needs a site for clients to tell them about your organization and what you have to offer. Then having a site is an excellent option for you.
    • Blog – A blog is a special type of website dedicated to sharing your thoughts, ideas, passion, tutorials, and many more. Blogs will display the most recently-published content first. Through you can follow your passion and also earn money and you can be your own boss of time and money.
    • E-learning site – online learning and online courses for the students, with the aim to track their growth from an e-learning website. You can offer online courses from a WordPress site with a special kind of plugin called a WordPress LMS plugin.
    • Portfolio site – you can just show off your designs, drawings, paintings, artwork, design skills, and much more with a portfolio site on WordPress.
    • Event site – if you are going host and event, a big event, or a blockbuster event then WordPress will make it easier for you to share your event details online and sell tickets of that particular event and earn money.

    WordPress.com and WordPress.org

    If you are a beginner person or new to this software then you may get confused between WordPress.com and WordPress.org. To clear this up, let’s get started with what WordPress is. WordPress is an open-source software package and is free to use. Anyone can use this software around the world.

    • org is the central location for the WordPress software project.
    • WordPress.com is a hosting service that offers websites that are running on WordPress. You can log in for a free account and work on your website.
